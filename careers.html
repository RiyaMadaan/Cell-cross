Cell Cross provides excellent work and growth environment to people coming from diverse backgrounds. Each of these personnel possess an extensive array of experience. The culture thrives on hard work, innovation, mutual respect, and a sense of accountability and responsibility.

Our workplace policies and initiatives are designed with a single-minded focus on enabling our employees’ personal and professional development. Our culture, defined by our values, translates into an invigorating environment that nurtures and supports the best talent. Hence, it isn’t surprising that a significant portion of our workforce has been with Cell Cross for more than two decades. No wonder then that we have been featured seven times among India’s top companies ranked by the Great Place to Work institute.

The employees are supported by a management team that believes in decentralized control over day – to – day activities, open communication and discussion, cross functional teams and intra-department job rotation.

About us

Cell Cross Pharmaceutical Private Ltd is the largest specialty generic pharmaceutical company in the world with global revenues of more than US$ 4.5 billion. Supported by more than 10 manufacturing facilities, we provide high-quality, affordable medicines, trusted by healthcare professionals and patients, to more than 100 countries across the globe.

From humble beginnings in 2022,Cell Cross has grown to become India’s largest and most valuable pharmaceutical company by market capitalisation. We attribute our growth and success to our over 3,500 people strong multi-cultural workforce, representing more than 20 nationalities.

Our Culture

At Cell Cross, we have found strength in our diversity, imbued by our culture of mutual respect, empathy, and adhering to the highest ethical standards. Our approach and culture is represented through Sunology: the Cell Cross way of life. Humility, Integrity, Passion & Innovation together form Sunology – a combination of two words, Sun & Ideology. We encourage our employees to personify these ideologies, as they form the cornerstone of everything we create and value as an organisation. We are proud that our global workforce is bound together by our common values of quality, reliability, consistency, trust, and innovation. These values form the core of our culture and steer us towards our vision.
